With the use of existing cameras within your facilities, Intenseye uses artificial intelligence to anonymously monitor the workplace, 24/7, sending real-time alerts of unsafe acts as they happen.

No hardware, no sensor, no installation

Our deep learning-powered AI models can predict and analyze body posture, ergonomics, danger zone violations, personal protective equipment usage in real-time. Based on our instant notification system facilities can stop violations before they turn into accidents.

Area Controls

One of the most common incidents that occur from crane or lifting equipment are workers being struck by an object. With the help of Intenseye, you can help prevent these incidents before they occur.

Behavioral Safety

Unsafe behavior accounts for 80% of accidents that occur in the workplace. Whether it's not holding onto a handrail or missing PPE, behavioral-based safety is an important part of safety protocol. With the use of Intenseye, you can identify these critical leading indicators and help prevent incidents before they occur.

Housekeeping

Whether it is slips, trips, falls or sharp objects thrown into trash bags, housekeeping maintenance is one of the most important safety measures in the workplace. Intenseye can detect anything from unattended objects to leakages and spills - creating alerts to prevent incidents before they occur.

Pandemic Controls

Covid-19 drastically changed the world as we once knew it and the residual effects on warehouse workers have forced industries to adopt a new normal. Continued social distancing, additional PPE, and new process and procedures are some of the changes we've seen. Intenseye can help ensure the safety of workers in a post-pandemic world.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

PPE is required in most warehouses to protect workers and minimize the exposure to hazards that can cause serious workplace injuries and illnesses. Often times, employers do not have visibility into the true compliance of PPE. Intenseye can see what was previously the unseen and provide a holistic picture of true compliance in the warehouse - all while protecting worker's privacy.

Vehicle Controls

Forklift incidents continue to be in the OSHA Top 10 citations. Whether it's overturns, workers falling off, or pedestrians struck, Intenseye can help prevent these incidents before they occur, all while protecting worker's privacy.

Worker Down

It could be a matter of seconds that changes the outcome of a life or death situation. In the event of an emergency, the information you have and how you react is critical. Intenseye can help ensure an efficient response to a man down emergency occurring in the workplace.
